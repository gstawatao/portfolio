Alexey Brodovitch: A Visual Story

Digital Piece, 2022

This piece, intended to be displayed in the form of a physical booklet, takes the viewer on a journey through famous designer, Alexey Brodovitch’s life and works. The booklet aims to tell Brodovitch’s story in his own style. Drawing inspiration from multiple of his works, this piece incorporates several of his techniques, pieces, and beliefs.
